Central Government Exams

The central government of India conducts a variety of exams for various departments and services. Below are some of the most popular exams.

UPSC Civil Services (IAS, IFS, IPS)

  • Age Limit: 21-32 years (age relaxation for SC/ST/OBC candidates)
  • Eligibility: Graduation in any stream from a recognized university.
  • Job Vacancies: Varies every year (approx. 1000-1200 vacancies)
  • Salary: ₹56,100 - ₹2,50,000 (depending on position)

SSC CGL (Combined Graduate Level)

  • Age Limit: 18-32 years
  • Eligibility: Graduation in any stream from a recognized university.
  • Job Vacancies: Varies (approximately 10,000 vacancies)
  • Salary: ₹25,500 - ₹81,100 (depending on post)

IBPS PO (Probationary Officer)

  • Age Limit: 20-30 years
  • Eligibility: Graduation in any discipline.
  • Job Vacancies: Around 4000 vacancies annually.
  • Salary: ₹35,000 - ₹40,000 (basic pay)

Indian Railway RRB

  • Age Limit: 18-30 years
  • Eligibility: 10th, 12th, or Graduation depending on the post.
  • Job Vacancies: Over 2,00,000 vacancies (varies every year)
  • Salary: ₹19,000 - ₹1,00,000 (depending on the post)

RBI Grade B Officer

  • Age Limit: 21-30 years
  • Eligibility: Graduation with a minimum of 60% marks.
  • Job Vacancies: Around 200-300 vacancies annually.
  • Salary: ₹35,000 - ₹60,000 (basic pay)

State Government Exams

Each state conducts its own set of exams for local government services, police departments, and other sectors. Below are some of the common state exams:

State Public Service Commission (SPSC)

  • Age Limit: 21-35 years (varies by state)
  • Eligibility: Graduation in any stream from a recognized university.
  • Job Vacancies: Varies by state (hundreds to thousands of vacancies)
  • Salary: ₹40,000 - ₹1,00,000 (depending on the position)

State Police Constable Exam

  • Age Limit: 18-25 years (varies by state)
  • Eligibility: 10th or 12th pass (depending on the state)
  • Job Vacancies: Varies by state (up to thousands of vacancies)
  • Salary: ₹20,000 - ₹40,000 (basic pay)

State Bank of India Clerk (SBI Clerk)

  • Age Limit: 20-28 years
  • Eligibility: Graduation in any discipline.
  • Job Vacancies: Around 8000 vacancies annually.
  • Salary: ₹19,900 - ₹47,920 (basic pay)

UPTET (Uttar Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test)

  • Age Limit: No specific age limit (depends on the recruitment notification)
  • Eligibility: Graduation with a B.Ed for TGT, and 12th pass for PGT positions.
  • Job Vacancies: Varies each year (typically in thousands)
  • Salary: ₹25,000 - ₹45,000 (depending on the role)
